1. Choose character

2. Choose map

3. Character spawns on map along with starting items/skills

4. Shop, trainer, and workshop spawn on map
  - skills trees, inventories, recipes, etc are separated by level

5. neutral creeps spawn on map
  - creeps are unique to locations, may have unique behavior,
    and may have random/unique drops
  - they do not initiate fights
  - players may initiate fights with them. Combat is a simple back-and-forth.
    Player expends action/stamina to fight.
